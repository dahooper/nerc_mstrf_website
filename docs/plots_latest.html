This page provides access to plots of the latest 24 hours' worth of data from instruments operated at the site. It is replacing the the Facility's supplementary website, which is not expected to exist beyond the early part of 2023. Note that:

  • the data have not undergone their final quality control checks
  • the MST Radar and LD40 plots are updated every 10 minutes. Plots of data from the other instruments are updated less frequently
  • these plots are being used to test colour blind friendly colour schemes
