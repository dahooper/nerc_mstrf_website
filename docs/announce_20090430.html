--- layout: default title: Announcement 2009-04-30 --- BBC at the MSTRF
A BBC crew filmed an interview with Prof Geraint Vaughan (University of Manchester) at the MST Radar site on 8th August 2008. This interview, together with footage of the radar site, featured prominently in a programme entitled "Wind" - the third of a three part series on the weather. The programmes were first broadcast on BBC4 between 13th and 27th April 2009. They are being repeated on BBC2 starting at 9 pm on Wednesday 29th April 2009. The "Wind" programme should therefore be broadcast on Wednesday 13th May.
