  • "Geoengineering, wind and sea squirts"

  • "Better landing procedures slash jet noise and pollution"
    NERC Planet Earth news article from September 2010. This highlights a refereed article, which made use of MST radar data (amongst others) in order to design advanced airport approach procedures for aircraft.
