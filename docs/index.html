The NERC MST Radar Facility at Aberystwyth:
  • is now known as the National Centre for Atmospheric Science (NCAS) Capel Dewi Atmospheric Observatory
  • is located at at 52.42°N, 4.01°W, in a rural valley near Aberystwyth in west Wales (UK)
  • operates a number of meteorological instruments, mainly in support of the National Centre for Atmospheric Science's (NCAS's) long term measurement programme
  • operates the 46.5 MHz Mesosphere-Stratosphere-Troposphere (MST) Radar, a wind-profiling instrument that may be booked for user-specific operations. The radar contributes to the long term measurement mode at all other times.
