The NERC MST Radar Facility at Aberystwyth:
  • is now known as the National Centre for Atmospheric Science (NCAS) Capel Dewi Atmospheric Observatory
  • is located at at 52.42°N, 4.01°W, in a rural valley near Aberystwyth in west Wales (UK)
  • operates and hosts a number of meteorological instruments in support of NCAS's long term measurement programme
  • makes its data openly-accessible through the Centre for Environmental Data Analysis (CEDA)
