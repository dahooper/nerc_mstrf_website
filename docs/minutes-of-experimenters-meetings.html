--- layout: default title: Minutes of experimenters' meetings ---

NERC MST Radar Facility Experimenters' meetings were typically held every 6 months. This page provides open access links to the minutes for meetings held between 2003 (the 32nd meeting) and 2023 (the 68th meeting). The minutes contain detailed records of presentations and discussions that typically followed an agenda:

1. Minutes of the previous meeting 2. Matters arising 3. Facility report 4. NERC instrument report 5. Guest instrument report 6. Science and technical presentations 7.
