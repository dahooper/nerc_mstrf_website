 THE NERC MST RADAR FACILITY AT ABERYSTWYTH
THE NEXT NERC MST RADAR FACILITY EXPERIMENTERS' MEETING
The 35th NERC MST Radar Facility Experimenters' Meeting will be held:
Date: Wednesday 8th December 2004 (the week before the UWERN conference)
Time: 09:00 - 15:00
Location: Penbryn Hall, The University of Wales Aberystwyth
It has been a busy summer for the Facility and so reports will be given on:
  • the new (multi-peak) radar signal processing
  • new instruments to be operated at the site
  • upgrades to the internet connection
Attendees are invited to give presentations on their research work which makes use of data from the Facility. A laptop running WindowsXP (with a video projector) and an overhead projector will be available.

If you would like to attend this meeting, please contact David Hooper, the NERC MST Radar Facility Project Scientist no later than 9 am on Wednesday 17th November together with:
  1. the title of any presentation that you would like to give
  2. any special dietary requirements
  3. whether you require overnight accommodation in Aberystwyth on Tuesday 7th November.
