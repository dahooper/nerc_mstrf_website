 THE NERC MST RADAR FACILITY AT ABERYSTWYTH
ANNOUNCEMENT 2011-02-28
THE MST RADAR IS OUT OF ACTION FOR RENOVATION WORK 28th FEBRUARY - 16th MARCH 2011
The MST Radar is undergoing its first major renovation work in its 20 year lifetime. The beam steering components, and some of the radio frequency cabling, are being replaced in order to improve the system's reliable and performance. If the istallation runs to schedule, the radar should be back in action by 16th March.
